As recorded in Article II of the ASA Constitution, the goals of the African Student Association are:

To promote an awareness and understanding of the various cultures, traditions and histories of the African diaspora.
To serve as a base of support and encouragement for people from Africa and within the club.
To discuss and develop solutions to the technological and infrastructure problems facing Africa.
To promote and help in the recruitment of underrepresented minorities.
To establish a network with the African community in the US.
